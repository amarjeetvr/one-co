Vardhman Mahavir Medical College (VMMC) is an eminent government medical institution set up in the year 2001, located in New Delhi, India. The college is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU). and is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

VMMC ranked 3rd by Collegedunia in 2026 in the Medical category. The course offerings include Bachelor of Science in Nursing (B.Sc Nursing), Bachelor of Medicine Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS), Doctor of Medicine (MD) among other undergraduate, postgraduate, and superspecialty nursing and Medical education courses. The admission process is through national level entrance exams - NEET UG, NEET PG and NEET SS with counselling managed by the Medical Counselling Committee (MCC).

Key Summary

Courses Offered: MBBS, B.Sc Nursing, MD, MS, DM, M.Ch and other UG, PG & Super-Specialty programs.

Admission: NEET UG for MBBS & B.Sc Nursing, NEET PG for MD/MS, and NEET SS for DM/M.Ch; IPU-CET is also accepted for certain courses.

Fees: Varies by program; admissions are strictly entrance-based with online applications.

Cutoff: Based on NEET UG, NEET PG, and NEET SS ranks (official cutoffs vary yearly as per MCC/AIQ rounds).

Placement: As per NIRF 2026, median packages were INR 15.84 LPA for MBBS and INR 19.20 LPA for PG programs. Top recruiters include AIIMS, Medanta, Fortis, Apollo, and Max Hospitals.

VMMC Admission 2026

VMMC Admission to major UG, PG, and Super Specialty courses such as MBBS/ B.Sc Nursing, M.D/ M.S, and D.M/ M.Ch is based on national-level entrance exams such as NEET UG, NEET PG, and NEET SS, respectively. Candidates first need to clear the respective entrance exam and then register for the counseling process conducted by the Medical Counseling Committee (MCC) for both State-Quota and All-India Quota Seats at VMMC.

The following video shares key details about Vardhman Mahavir Medical College and Safdarjung Hospital. It focuses on the courses offered, infrastructure, clinical exposure, and admission details of VMMC.

How to Apply for VMMC Admission 2026?

Here are the steps to apply for MCC NEET Counselling for VMMC Admission 2024: 

  • Visit the official website of MCC @ https://mcc.nic.in.
  • Click on the link for ‘New Candidate Registration’.
  • Enter the Roll Number, Name, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, and Security Pin.
  • Pay the non-refundable NEET Counseling Registration Fees. 
  • Submit the preferences by filling in the choices based on the NEET score.
  • Enter the password and click on ‘Lock Choices’ once done.

The candidates who are allotted a seat for admission to the desired course at Vardhman Mahavir Medical College need to report to the college with the necessary documents for document verification, fee payment, and completion of admission formalities.

VMMC Results

Vardhman Mahavir Medical College releases the results for various semesters and annual examinations for different courses on the official website of GGSIPU. The candidates can check the result directly in a PDF format without any login credentials. 

How to Check VMMC Result?

The steps to check VMMC Results are as follows: 

  • Visit the official website of VMMC. (https://www.vmmc-sjh.nic.in/)
  • Scroll down the homepage and check the ‘Exam Results’ section.
  • Click on the ‘Click Here for Exam Results - GGSIP University’ link.
  • The page will be directed to the Exam Results section of GGSIPU.
  • A page with all the latest announced results will appear on the screen. 
  • Search and click on the link for the desired course result. 
  • A PDF of the result will open. 
  • Check the result and download the PDF for future reference.

VMMC FAQs

Ques. Is Vardhman Mahavir Medical College a government college?

Ans. Yes, Vardhman Mahavir Medical College is a government college established by the Government of India in the year 2001. The college operates under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India.

Ques. What is the ranking of Vardhman Mahavir Medical College?

Ans. Vardhman Mahavir Medical College has secured the 17th  position in the NIRF 2024 Rankings in the Medical Category. The college has also been ranked 7th   by India Today in 2025 under the medical category.

Ques. What are the courses offered by VMMC Delhi?

Ans. VMMC Delhi offers courses at Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Super Specialty levels in the field of Medicine and Nursing. The major courses offered by VMMC are: 

  • MBBS
  • B.Sc (Hons) Nursing
  • M.D
  • M.S
  • D.M
  • M.Ch

Ques. Who are the top recruiters at VMMC Placements?

Ans. The top recruiters at VMMC Placements are: 

  • Apollo
  • Fortis Healthcare
  • Max Hospital
  • AIIMS
  • Medanta
  • PGIMER

Ques. Does VMMC Delhi offer hostel accommodation?

Ans. Yes, VMMC Delhi offers hostel accommodation for both girls and boys. There are two boys’ hostels and one girls’ hostel at Vardhman Mahavir Medical College with an attached mess, sports facilities, and a TV room.

Ques. Does VMMC Delhi offer internship opportunities?

Ans. VMMC Delhi organizes internship programs for internal as well as external students at Safdarjung Hospital. MBBS degree holders can apply by registering themselves with the Delhi Medical Council of India and sending all the required self-attested documents to the Principal of VMMC & SJH.

Ques. Is hostel accommodation available for all students at VMMC?

Ans. VMMC does not guarantee hostel accommodation for all students. It serves the hostel facility on a first come first basis. VMMC prioritizes MBBS students coming from a non-Delhi region for hostel accommodations and considers other students later in case any seat is left.

Ques. What are the entrance exams accepted by VMMC Delhi?

Ans. Vardhman Mahavir Medical College offers admission to major UG, PG, and Super Specialty courses such as MBBS/B.Sc Nursing, M.D/M.S, and D.M/M.Ch on the basis of national-level entrance exams such as NEET UG, NEET PG, and NEET SS respectively.

Ques. What are the facilities offered by VMMC Delhi?

Ans. Vardhman Mahavir Medical College has an excellent IT Infrastructure and campus with facilities such as

  • Hostels
  • Cafeteria
  • Convenience Store
  • Hospital/Medical Facilities
  • Labs
  • Library
  • Sports Complex and Gym

Ques. How many seats are offered for MBBS at VMMC?

Ans. VMMC offers 170 seats for admission to the MBBS course at the undergraduate level. 

Ques. Is VMMC recognized by the Medical Council of India?

Ans. Yes, Vardhman Mahavir Medical College is recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).

Ques. What are the specializations offered for the M.D. course at VMMC?

Ans. VMMC offers the M.D. course in various specializations namely Anesthesiology, Pediatrics, General Medicine, Pathology, Radiodiagnosis, Microbiology, Community Medicine, Physiology, Pharmacology, Psychiatry, Biochemistry, Dermatology, Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation, Sports Medicine, Forensic Medicine, and Anatomy.

Ques. Which is better AIIMS or VMMC?

Ans. AIIMS Delhi is ranked 1st in the NIRF Ranking 2023 in the Medical Category while VMMC is ranked at 14th position. AIIMS Delhi is undoubtedly the best choice for students seeking admission to MBBS, however, in case the student is not able to meet the cutoff for AIIMS, then, VMMC is the best option as it is a renowned college in India. 

Ques. What are the specializations offered in MS at VMMC?

Ans. VMMC offers MS in all major specializations which are as follows:

  • ENT
  • General Surgery
  • Orthopedics
  • Obstetrics and Gynecology
  • Opthalmology

Ques. What is the average package offered at VMMC?

Ans. According to VMMC NIRF Report 2023, the average package offered to MBBS and PG students at VMMC Placements stood at INR 1.5 LPA, and INR 1 LPA, respectively. 

Ques. What is the cutoff for MBBS at VMMC Delhi?

Ans. VMMC NEET Cutoff is different for State Quota and All-India Quota. The closing NEER rank for VMMC MBBS Cutoff 2023 for admission to MBBS is 113 for All-India Quota and 1556 for State-Quota for the General Category.

Ques. What is the admission criteria for B.Sc Nursing at VMMC?

Ans. Admission to B.Sc Nursing at VMMC is based on the score in NEET UG. Rest of the seats left after MCC Counselling are filled through the counselling by GGSIPU. 

Ques. How many students are placed in VMMC Placements?

Ans. 15 MBBS students were placed during VMMC 2021 Placements, while 202 students of PG courses were placed during VMMC 2022 Placements as per VMMC NIRF Report 2023. 

Ques. Is VMMC affiliated with Delhi University?

Ans. No, VMMC is not affiliated with Delhi University. The college is affiliated with the Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), New Delhi.

Ques. How many seats are offered for B.Sc Nursing at VMMC?

Ans. VMMC offers a total of 50 Seats for admission to its B.Sc Nursing course. 

Ques. Which hospital is VMMC associated with?

Ans. VMMC Delhi is associated with the famous Safdarjung Hospital in Delhi for the clinical teaching and learning of students. 

Ques. Does VMMC offer residency posts?

Ans. Yes, VMMC offers residency posts to both junior and senior residents. Students pursuing M.D. or M.S. are known as resident doctors. They are called resident doctors because they have their medical graduate degrees and are now engaged in medical practices for the experience. They are available 24 x 7 at the hospital.

How is the internship (after MBBS) at VMMC Safdarjung Hospital, Delhi?

Akriti Banerjeelives in New Delhi

The internship scenario at VMMC is quite disappointing and tiring. The number of patients is so huge in all the departments and interns have to work more than required to get the work done. Their focus is not to learn new procedures but to complete the work and go. Here is the department-wise scenario 

  • Pediatrics : In this department, interns are required to find all the case sheets(files) and reports and arrange them before the rounds begin. They are also responsible for dextrose monitoring and discharges. If you are posted in labor, you will have to ensure the baby’s safety. 
  • Surgery : Here, interns are required to do sampling, dressing, and discharges. In emergency minor OT, you will have to I n D, suturing, catheterization, and such. Interns also go to OT to watch the surgeries. Postgraduate students may let you do procedures like hydrocele on minor OT days.
  • Medicine : The patient load varies from season to season. In dengue season, it’s maximum and you will always be busy doing sampling and cannulation.
  • OBC : Here you are responsible for sampling, cannulation, catheterization, and NST. You can also take deliveries in the labor room.
  • Orthopedic : During emergency days you will be busy with cast and traction. You might also have to do skeletal traction.
  • For other departments like PSM, ENT, Derma, Optha, and Casualty, the workload is comparatively less so you get a lot of free time. 

Overall, through the internships, you will get confident at least in minor tasks, and gain a lot of hands-on experience.
