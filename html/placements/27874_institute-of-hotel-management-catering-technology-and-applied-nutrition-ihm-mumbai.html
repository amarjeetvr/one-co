IHM Mumbai Placements: Students' Opinion

AI-Generated Summary based on 77 Reviews

IHM Mumbai boasts a strong placement record, with approximately 90-95% of students securing employment through campus placements. The recruitment process begins in the third year, primarily during the 5th and 6th semesters, where prestigious hotel chains like Taj, Oberoi, ITC, Marriott, and Hyatt conduct interviews. Salary packages typically range from INR 2.5 to 3.5 lakhs per annum, with the potential to reach up to INR 6.7 lakhs based on roles and experience.

The college offers robust internship opportunities in the second year, with placements in top-tier hotels both domestically and internationally. Stipends for internships vary, ranging from INR 1,000 to 8,000 per month, with international placements offering more. The institution supports students in securing both placements and internships, emphasizing skill development and industry exposure.

IHM Mumbai: 1 Answered Question

What salary could one expect from placements from IHM Pusa, Mumbai and Goa?

Megh DasguptaB.Sc. from Institute of Hotel Management, Catering Technology and Applied Nutrition, Mumbai (2016)

Although the initial salary packages offered by IHMs are good, don’t expect a cushy package. Your salary package will depend on the company that you are going to work for. It can range between INR 15,000 - INR 30,000/month. You can also get increments depending on the company rules. You should prepare thoroughly for the interview. 

If you go through the management trainee program for some hostel you will get around INR 20,000 - INR 24,000. After completing the 2-year long trainee program you can expect to get a salary package of INR 40,000 - INR 50,000/month.

If you go through the management trainee program for food companies like KFC, Dominos, Starbucks then you can expect to earn around INR 25,000 for 2 years. After the training period, you will get salary packages around INR 30,000 - INR 40,000/month.  The retail company job profiles also have a similar pay. 

So, first, you should complete your management trainee program from a company that way you can start easily.
