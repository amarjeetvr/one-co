Miranda House Placement Statistics FAQs

Ques. How is the placement process at Miranda House?

Ans. Miranda House Placement Cell invites companies to conduct pre-placement talks, job descriptions about salaries and other emoluments, as well as growth opportunities that a company might provide. The selection process generally comprises an aptitude test, group discussion, and personal interviews. Offer letters are rolled out immediately after the completion of the selection, guiding students guided by the Placement Cell to make informed decisions.

Ques. Do a lot of students at Miranda House prefer continuing their studies over accepting job placements?

Ans. Yes, a large number of students from the Miranda House go for postgraduate studies, research programs, or competitive examinations. That is probably one reason the placement rate looks relatively modest despite a college recruiter list that reads excellent, because a percentage of students have more professionalism in getting higher education.

Miranda House Placement FAQs

Ques. Can students at Miranda House get internships as well as final placements?

Ans. Yes, Miranda House lays very strong emphasis on internships as a part of its placement ecosystem. Firms of such great repute coming to offer summer internships and live projects to undergraduates include EY, ICICI Bank, and Deloitte. Mostly students avail this opportunity after completing their second year. Internships, apart from providing real industry exposure to the student, go a long way in enhancing the possibility of getting a job offer-Pre Placement Offer (PPO) if the student performs well during the internship.

Ques. Which main sectors hire from Miranda House?

Ans. Recruiters at Miranda House belong to varied industries. Though companies falling under the Banking, Finance, and Consulting sector form a major portion, there is good representation of IT, Analytics, Media, Education, and FMCG industries as well. For instance, McKinsey & Co. and EY are regular recruiters for consulting profiles, whereas Wipro and TCS provide IT-based jobs. Such diversity makes sure that ample opportunities are available for students belonging to both science and arts backgrounds

Ques. How does Miranda House help students get ready for placements?

Ans. The Placement Cell in Miranda House has initiated a series of training sessions for the students to enable them to face interviews and selection processes with much confidence. Schedules under this include resume-building workshops, mock aptitude tests, case-study practices, group discussions, as well as mock personal interviews. Alumni working in reputed organisations also come back to conduct mentorship sessions, further enlightening the students on industry expectations and pathways that can be taken in their careers. Such organised preparation leads directly to an enhanced placement success rate.

Miranda House Placements: Students' Opinion

AI-Generated Summary based on 277 Reviews

Miranda House has an active placement cell that facilitates both internships and job placements across various domains. Students from the final year are eligible for campus placements, with companies like Accenture, BYJU's, Dell, and Tech Mahindra frequently visiting. The highest package recorded is INR 21.5 LPA, with an average package of around INR 8.97 LPA. Placement opportunities are notably better for students in Economics, Mathematics, and Science courses, while arts students generally pursue higher studies.

Approximately 65-87% of students receive placements, depending on the course. The placement cell also provides numerous internship opportunities, with stipends ranging from INR 3,000 to 80,000. Overall, despite the college not having a strong placement record compared to technical institutions, it offers significant exposure and career opportunities, particularly in internships and for students who excel academically.
