NLSIU Bangalore B.A.LL.B. (Hons.) Selection Process 2026

  1. Appeared for CLAT UG 2026.
  2. Apply through the CLAT Consortium official portal within the registration window.
  3. Shortlisting based on CLAT All India Rank (AIR).
  4. Participate in CLAT counselling and seat allotment.
  5. Report to NLSIU Bangalore for admission formalities.

NLSIU Bangalore Ph.D. Admission 2026

NLSIU Bangalore offers a Ph.D. program in Law and Interdisciplinary Studies, focusing on advanced research in legal theory, constitutional studies, governance, human rights, and related fields.

Eligibility requires a Master’s degree in Law (LL.M.) with at least 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PwD) or a Master’s degree in another discipline relevant to research. Admission is based on NLSAT-Research / UGC NET / JRF, followed by an interview and proposal evaluation.

Particulars Details
Program Name Doctor of Philosophy in Law (Ph.D.)
Duration Minimum 3 Years, Maximum 6 Years
Entrance Exam Accepted NLSAT
Selection Process Written Test → Research Proposal Evaluation → Personal Interview
Application Mode Online
Course Fees INR 1.90 Lakhs
Eligibility Criteria LL.M. with 55% marks (50% for SC/ST/PwD) OR M.Phil. in Law

NLSIU Bangalore Ph.D. Selection Process 2026

  1. Register & Apply on NLSIU Portal.
  2. Appear for NLSAT-Research (if not NET/JRF qualified).
  3. Submit Research Proposal.
  4. Attend an Interview.
  5. Final selection based on proposal + interview.

NLSIU Bangalore Ph.D. Documents Required

  • Research Proposal (Detailed)

NLSIU Bangalore Admission 2026

National Law School of India University (NLSIU), Bangalore admits students through CLAT, CLAT-PG, or NLSAT, depending on the programme. Admissions are merit-based, subject to eligibility, reservations, and seat availability, and finalized through the prescribed counselling process.

NLSIU Bangalore Admission Process 2026

Stage Details
Entrance Exam Registration Register and appear for the relevant entrance exam — CLAT UG (BA LL.B.), CLAT PG (LL.M.), or NLSAT (LL.B., MPP, Ph.D., and select programmes).
Application to NLSIU Apply through the CLAT Consortium counselling portal or the NLSIU admission portal, depending on the programme.
Counselling / Shortlisting Participate in centralized counselling for CLAT-based programmes or institute-level shortlisting and interviews for NLSAT-based programmes.
Seat Allotment Seats are allotted based on entrance scores, rank, category, programme preferences, and seat availability.
Seat Acceptance & Fee Payment Confirm admission by completing document verification and paying the prescribed admission fee within the deadline.
Ph.D. Selection Process Ph.D. applicants must clear the written test, submit a research proposal, and appear for a personal interview conducted by National Law School of India University.

NLSIU Admission FAQs

Ques. Is there a domicile quota for Karnataka students at NLSIU?

Ans. No, NLSIU does not have a state domicile quota. Admissions are open to candidates from all states based on merit and applicable reservation norms.

Ques. Can someone change programme preference after submitting the application?

Ans. Programme preferences can be changed only during the official correction or counselling window. Changes are not allowed once the window closes.

Ques. Are gap years accepted for admission to law programmes?

Ans. Yes, gap years are permitted if the candidate meets the eligibility criteria and qualifies the required entrance examination.

How should I prepare for NLSIU Bangalore?

ShrutiStudent at NLSIU Bangalore

Since NLSIU Bangalore is currently the top NLU in India, with an NIRF rank of #1, you will have to put in a lot of work. There will be 120 questions in the exam overall. The questions will be based on the updated CLAT Exam Pattern. Here's how you can prepare for CLAT to get into NLSIU Bangalore:

  • Practise mock tests: Practise at least 1-2 mock tests of all portions of CLAT each day, to examine your performance and know your weaker areas.
  • Practice PYQs: In a similar manner, practise previous years’ CLAT questions to understand the exam pattern and type of questions asked. You can also get an idea of the difficulty level of the exam, and the questions that are often repeated.

  • Apps: CLAT 2025 LLB Law Exam Prep, CLAT Essentials by EducationDIY Media, CLAT 2024 Exam Preparation App by AILET Law Entrance.
  • Test Series: CLAT Free Mock Tests 2025 by LegalEdge, CLAT Mock Test 2024 Free by Byju’s, CLAT Mock Test 2024 Free by iexambee.

Can I get admission in any NLSIU after cracking CLAT?

Rytash AnandBTS from Indira Gandhi National Open University (2020)

Presently, only one college comes under the NLSIU tag and it is number one on the list. In order to get into NLSIU Bangalore, you have to make sure that your rank falls between AIR 1 to AIR 30. 

Surely, there are other colleges as well that you can get into after cracking CLAT, the ones with the NLU and NALSAR tag. Here are some facts you should know about NLU.

  • NLUs are allotted as per your rank and your preference list which you indicate in your application.
  • For example, if you have an AIR 1, and have chosen NLSIU Bangalore as your first preference, you will get into NLSIU Bangalore and other colleges mentioned in the first place of your list.
  • But if you get an AIR between 500-600s and NLSIU is your first preference, there is no chance of getting into NLSIU and you may end up in a lower-ranked NLUs.
  • If your AIR is between 1 to 40 you can get into any National Law University of your preference.

Therefore try working harder for your CLAT exam, as the better your rank will get you admitted in your preferred colleges.
