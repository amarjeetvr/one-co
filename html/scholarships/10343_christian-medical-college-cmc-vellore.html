CMC Vellore Scholarships: Students' Opinion

AI-Generated Summary based on 42 Reviews

Christian Medical College offers a range of scholarships and financial aid to economically disadvantaged students, ensuring accessibility to quality medical education. The total course fee is approximately INR 2,65,330, with tuition fees around INR 65,000, divided into INR 50,000 for tuition, INR 8,000 refundable caution money, and INR 1,500 for library fees. Hostel accommodations cost INR 6,400 per month, plus a minimum mess charge of INR 3,000 monthly. For students with a family income below INR 1 lakh, scholarships and fee concessions are available, often covering tuition and living expenses.

Internal financial aid of INR 18,000 annually requires a one-year service commitment. The Ministry of Human Resource Development also facilitates National and External Scholarships for eligible students. Additionally, the college has arrangements with local banks for student loans and provides payment during internships. Financial support is contingent on an income certificate from authorized government bodies, potentially waiving up to 100% of fees for deserving candidates.
